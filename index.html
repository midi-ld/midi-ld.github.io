MIDI Linked Data

MIDI Linked Data is the representation of symbolic music in MIDI format following the Web data publishing principles of Linked Data. Join us and contribute to extending the MIDI Linked Data Cloud!

MIDI Linked Data Cloud

The MIDI Linked Data Cloud connects all MIDI files in the world in a giant RDF graph of interconnected MIDI statements. Songs are connected among themselves through their shared resources (e.g. same instruments, same notes), and to the rest of the Web through common metadata (e.g. song names, authors).

What does it buy you?

For semantics: what does it mean that a song transmits sadness? What do all blues songs have in common? For music information retrieval: can we conceal the various music annotation methods following Web standards? Can we search music combining both metadata and musical features? For interlinked musical databases: can we make MusicXML, MIDI, MEI, incipit databases interoperable?
